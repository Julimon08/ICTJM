Recomendations

I am a person that when people ask to recommend something I will always recommend books; I will recommend you 3 of my favorite books, I really think they are worthy reading and a person that like the drama and the police books will enjoy a lot. The books that I will recommend are: - El Jardín de las mariposas: it is a thriller full of drama and a lot of real live scenarios (things that actually happen nowadays) and is about a girl that is telling the story of how a person called “el Jardinero” kidnapped her and other girls. It is a very good book and I promise it is worthy to read it. - ALL the saga of Shadow Hunters: This is my favorite book saga (it is just perfect) the book is developed in a way that is very interesting and the “world” of this book is very organized. There are a lot of sagas in this saga but the principal one is about 18-year-old Clary Fray, who finds out on her birthday that she is not who she thinks, and her mom gets kidnapped by a guy named Valentine. She discovers that she comes from a long line of Shadowhunters (human-angel hybrids who hunt down demons). - La chica de la nieve: It is a very interesting book full of mystery and drama. It is about a family in 1998 in a Christmas show and when the Dad and the little girl go to take a balloons the girl (3 years old) gets lost. 5 years after the girl was declared missing the parents receive a video of the girl (now 8 years old) playing in a unknown room.
